1. Business Metrics
	i. KPI 1: Total Customers:

		Total Customers = Number of Customers

	ii. KPI 2: Churned Customers:
		
		Customers with Churn = Yes

	iii. KPI 3: Churn Rate:

		Churn Rate= (Churned Customers/Total Customers)*100

	iv. KPI 4: Retention Rate:

		Retention Rate= 100 − Churn Rate
